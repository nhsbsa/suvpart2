SUV Re-design

Version 1

As per the business process, a new re-design of SUV is required to allow for an improved accessibe version under the new NHS Toolkit.

Presently, the SUV system has various funtions that all appear in a pop-up window which is not considered accessible.

This prototype explores the function of a pop-up function removed and replaced by a seperate page function.

There is an individual Page list, which contains each individual SUV page.

Version 3

In this version of the prototype we have added in a Direct Debit journey. We are to gather more info regarding creating a refund note and flagging a customer as vulnerable.

After a demo run through with the business, a baseline prototype was drafted thus ready for testing.

An in-person testing session was organised with call centre staff on the 19/09/2023 to run through this version of the prototype to see if the design aligned with user mental models.

Version 5

This version of the prototype has the Direct Debit journey integrated, with 5 slightly different variations of the Direct Debit journey.

Prototype journeys

Option 1

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (CCS user).

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (Back office user).

For this version, the right hand column of the Direct Debit journey is changed on the initial set up screen, and also on the enter your bank details screen to show the personal information rather than the PCN information. The rest of the journey remains unchanged.

Option 2

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (CCS user).

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (Back office user).

For this version, the right hand column on the initial set up screen and the enter your bank details screen have changed to show personal details at the top, and a new tab at the bottom to show the PCN information that was replaced. The rest of the journey remains unchanged.

Option 2.1

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (CCS user).

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (Back office user).

For this version, the right hand column will show personal details at the top throughout the entire journey, and a new tab at the bottom to show the PCN information.

Option 3

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (CCS user).

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (Back office user).

For this version, it is essentially 2.1 but 'flipped', the top of the right hand column remains as PCN information, with the introduction of a new tab at the bottom of the right hand column showing the personal information throughout the journey.

Option 4

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (CCS user).

Direct Debit journey integrated into SUV (Back office user).

This option is a 'curve ball' in the sense it is a slight re-design implementing the newer tabs component and the right hand column visually looking more inline with newer card component in the toolkit. This deisgn has PCN information and personal information as two tabs towards top of the right hand column, and the notes and transactions at the bottom of the right hand column. There is also another slight change, a new 'row' at the top of the column encapsulating the reference number so it is always visible and a 'tag' component to show the status. These changes are throughout the entire journey.
